02

Travel

Getting to Mazunte takes a little effort — and that's part of the charm. The closest airport is Huatulco (HUX), about a 90-minute drive east along the coast. Puerto Escondido (PXM) is a shorter drive in the other direction. Oaxaca City and Mexico City are larger hubs if you need more flight options.

05

Activities

Mazunte and its neighbors — Zipolite, San Agustinillo, Ventanilla — offer everything from quiet beach mornings to bioluminescent night swims, mezcal tastings, sea turtle releases, and the sunset hike up Punta Cometa.
