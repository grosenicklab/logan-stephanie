The Wedding

We're getting married in Mazunte, the small town on the Pacific coast of southern Mexico where we met each other. The wedding runs four days, January 7 to 10, 2027.

Note rooms at the main hotel are limited and later bookers will have to stay offsite, so please RSVP by August 1st. If you'll need more time to decide or any help, let us know!

02

Travel

Getting to Mazunte takes some effort. Fly through Mexico City (MEX) to either Huatulco (HUX) or Puerto Escondido (PXM), then taxi to Mazunte.

East side
Huatulco (HUX)
about 45 min taxi from Mazunte
West side
Puerto Escondido (PXM)
about an hour taxi from Mazunte

Ground transport details and shared shuttle options will follow.

Activities
